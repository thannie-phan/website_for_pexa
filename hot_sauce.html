About

Find my fruit hot sauces at The Tasty Stuff

The Tasty Stuff, essentially, is just the perfect blend of fruits, vegetables and chillies (with a wee bit of spice, sugar and salt to amplify their natural flavours, making the sauce really TASTY).

We're really traditional. Even the recipes for our hot sauces are a family heritage (written in the year 2023). Okay, that's a joke, but we promise we're serious about our ingredients.

We're 100% plant-based and 100% natural, with NO preservatives or additives.

Our flavours include:

  • Habanero Berry
  • Habanero Passionfruit
  • Habanero Apricot
  • Jalapeno Kiwi

*As we're all natural, separation may occur. Fret not! Shake well before use and you're good to go!

Why fruit hot sauce is the best?

  • A quicker alternative to bungee jumping on your lunch break for some adrenaline.
  • Fruity, sweet & tangy with a little bit of heat. Makes every bite better.
  • Goes with anything and everything, from smashed avo to charcoal chicken, even ice cream!
